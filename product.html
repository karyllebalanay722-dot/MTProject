Products of San Nicolas, Ilocos Norte

Damili (Pottery)

San Nicolas is famous for it's pottery, a tradition that dates back to the Spanish colonial era. The clay products, known as damili, include various pots like Banga, traditional stoves or we called it Dalikan, and plant pot.

Banga (Clay Pot)

Dalikan (Clay Stove)

Other Product of San Nicolas

San Nicolas is a historic center for blacksmithing in the Philippines, where the craft is passed down through families. Traditional products: Blacksmiths in the area (known locally as "panday") create a variety of iron implements, including agricultural tools like sickles (kumpay) and plows (arado), as well as knives like the bolo.
