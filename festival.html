DAMILI FESTIVAL


In San Nicolas, Ilocos Norte, December is a time for giving thanks and celebrating through the Damili Festival. This month-long event includes dances, parades, beauty contests, talent shows, trade fairs, exhibits, and sports games. The main event happens in the last week, called the Dance Pageantry. It shows the town's old pottery tradition, called "damili". People dance in the streets to honor this craft, using props like clay pots, tools, and animals. Each village joins the dance contest, wearing colorful costumes. The Ilocano song “Agdamdamili” plays, showing the people's hard work, kindness, and strong spirit through good times and bad.
